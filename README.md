# Family Law Growth Partners Vercel Site

This is a ready-to-deploy Next.js site for Vercel.

## Deploy in Vercel
1. Log in to Vercel.
2. Click **Add New** → **Project**.
3. Choose the option to import or upload this folder/repository.
4. Click **Deploy**.
5. After the site is live on the temporary Vercel URL, go to **Project Settings** → **Domains**.
6. Add your Network Solutions domain.
7. Copy the DNS records Vercel gives you into Network Solutions.

## Important placeholders to replace
- `hello@familylawgrowthpartners.com`
- `(555) 555-5555`
- Any copy you want made more specific to your offer
